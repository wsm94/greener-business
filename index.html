EMS Benefits

To be truly effective we believe an EMS should benefit you, the environment, and all of your customers, suppliers and stakeholders.

Go Greener

Developing an EMS will help reduce your organisation's impact on the environment, both locally and globally.

Improve Sustainability

Environmentally focused business are prepared for the future and changing consumer demands.

Save Money

It's now often cheaper to 'go-green' in many parts of your business. See how much money you could save just by using our lighting survey.

Access New Markets

57% of consumers are now willing to pay more for an environmentally-friendly product. Many public contracts require an EMS to tender and B2B is moving the same way.

Improve Employee Motivation & Engagement

Many top-tier employees, particularly those entering the workforce now, would prefer to work for an environmentally-concious organisation.

Let Your Customers Know

Accreditation and/or publishing parts of your EMS proves to your customers that you take being a Greener Business Seriously.
